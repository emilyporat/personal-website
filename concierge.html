Background

Orchard's Concierge program is a renovation service for customers who want to make improvements to their home before selling in order to increase list price and sell faster. Orchard pays for the renovation upfront and customers pay us back once their home sells.

Why invest in Concierge?

Concierge is important to Orchard's business for two main reasons:

  • It drives conversion and sets Orchard apart from its competitors, especially in a buyer's market when selling is tougher.
  • Orchard introduced a 35% Concierge program fee in late 2022, which contributes to overall profitability.

Many Concierge workflows were still being done manually in 2022, which resulted in operational inefficiency and financial losses due to human error.

Problem statement

How might we improve internal tooling for managing the Concierge program to ensure data accuracy and improve internal efficiency?

The project

Primary users

  • Renovation Leads (RLs) are the main user group — they are internal Orchard employees who manage Concierge projects, including scoping out the work to be done, sending contracts to customers, and ensuring the work is completed.
  • RLs spend roughly 70% of their time in the field, visiting job sites, and 30% of their time doing computer work.

How were RLs managing Concierge before?

Most were using some combination of a free-text box in Salesforce, bespoke personal spreadsheets, and other external tools like Buildertrend. Because Concierge details weren't being tracked programatically, the Concierge Coordinator also had to generate invoices manually using a GSheet template.

Design process

  • Current state audit: Reviewed existing tooling and identified product gaps & irrelevant info.
  • Field research: A PM and I traveled to Austin to shadow a Renovation Lead and see what a day in his life looked like.
  • Low fi design & user flow testing: Created mockups and tested with RLs to get feedback.
  • High-fi design: created new design system components and created a polished finished product.
  • Eng review & visual QA: worked with tech lead to identify new components, answer questions, and review flow once built out.

Project scope

We added net-new functionality to:

  • Generate contracts
  • Send contract amendments when the projects change
  • View contract statuses and a summary of key details
  • Generate invoices based on the latest contract

Outcome

The new & improved Concierge feature launched in Jan 2023 and was received very well.

  • Accuracy — Concierge-related financial escalations decreased 80% in the 3 following quarters post-launch.
  • Operational efficiency — 100% of RLs polled post-launch said that the new product enabled them to do their job more efficiently.
  • Conversion + profitability — These improvements allowed RLs to focus on providing a great customer experience and spend less time struggling with inadequate tooling.

The Renovation Leads were also very happy with the rollout.

  • Internal CSAT for Concierge tooling rose from 3.78 to 4.6 (+22%)!
  • In a post-launch survey, one RL said: "I have already used it to create my first scope...and it is Awesome! I no longer have to use Excel!!! Great work!"
  • Another: "The new ability to redraft and update the concierge agreement is a smashing success! One of the best improvements I have seen in all software platforms we used in the 3+ years I have been here."
