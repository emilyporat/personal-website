Concierge

Introduced full product support for Orchard's "Concierge" renovation program.

Project Overview

I led research & design on the Concierge project, which introduced net new functionality to Orchard's internal platform for project planning, generating smart contracts, and sending invoices.

Start date

Oct 2022

Release date

Jan 2023

Team

Me, PM, Tech lead, 2 other engineers

Background

  • Orchard offers streamlined services and tooling for consumers looking to buy and/or sell their home.
  • The Concierge program is Orchard's renovation service for customers who want to make improvements to their home before selling in order to increase list price and decrease days on market.

The project

  • Renovation Leads (RLs) are the main user group — they are internal Orchard employees who manage renovation projects, including scoping out the work to be done, sending contracts to customers, and ensuring the work is completed. RLs spend roughly 60% of their time in the field, visiting job sites, and 40% of their time doing computer work.
  • Previously, RLs had to manually create contracts and were using their own hacky spreadsheets and notes docs to write out scopes of work.
  • This project enabled Renovation Leads to generate and send smart contracts to customers via the product and track the status of those contracts. We also introduced the ability to generate invoices once the work is complete.

Why it matters

  • This functionality will decrease financial escalations related to data entry mistakes and drastically improve operational efficiency.
  • Concierge is also an important driver to conversion (Concierge attach rates doubled from 20% in 2021 to 40% in 2022) and profitability (Orchard introduced a 35% program fee and asset management fees in late 2022).

Primary flow

Sending a contract to a customer
