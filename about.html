About Emily

Hey! I'm Emily. I'm an NYC-based product designer.

I currently work at Orchard, a real estate startup that offers streamlined services and tooling for consumers looking to buy and/or sell their home. I lead design on our Platform team, which supports all internal tooling for Orchard's agents and operations teams. In my 2+ years at Orchard, I've worked on projects big & small, from redesigning a global search feature to overhauling our entire platform design system to introducing net-new customer management dashboards.

Before Orchard, I worked in gov consulting at Deloitte Digital, where I led a mix of redesign and research projects for a variety of clients and industries, ranging from mobile apps to consumer websites to interntal tools. I also started a Junior Designer mentorship program from scratch, where I led programming and created resources for 40+ aspiring designers.

I built my foundation in design at Carnegie Mellon, where I studied Information Systems and Human-Computer Interaction. I was also a teaching assistant for an Intro to CS course and worked on a HCI research project called Zensors.

In my free time, you can catch hanging with my cats (Bean & Basil), cooking elaborate dinners for friends, and reading.
